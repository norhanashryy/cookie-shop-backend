 Get product by ID

Admin:
 Create product
 Update product

        id,
        name,
        description,
        price,
        stock,
        imageUrl,

    }

📦 Orders
Logged-in users:
- Place order
- View my orders

Admin:
- View all orders
- Change order status

Statuses:
- Pending
- Preparing
- Out for Delivery
- Delivered
- Cancelled

👮 Authorization
 Normal users cannot create products.
 Admins can manage products.
 Users can only view their own orders.
